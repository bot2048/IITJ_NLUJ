2-DAY National Workshop on LAW & TECHNOLOGY

Second Edition

Jointly Organised By
National Law University Jodhpur & Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur

A dialogue on how law and technology can together shape ethical, inclusive, and future-ready societies.

Schedule Overview

Day 1 - April 05, 2025

Venue: Senate Room, IIT Jodhpur

9:30 AM - 11:15 AM

Inauguration

11:15 AM - 11:45 AM

High Tea

11:45 AM - 1:00 PM

AI-assisted Decision Making in the Indian Justice/Judicial Systems

Delve into AI-driven applications in the justice system, focusing on efficiency and accessibility.

Discuss the ethical considerations and potential biases of AI in legal decision-making.

Explore the use of AI for:

• Summary generation and transliteration of legal documents

• Precedent identification and case analysis

• Predictive policing and crime prevention

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Lunch

2:00 PM - 3:45 PM

Cybersecurity in the Age of Emerging Technologies

Address the evolving landscape of cybercrime and cyber forensics.

Discuss the legal frameworks governing cybersecurity and data protection in India.

Explore the role of technology in preventing and mitigating cyber threats.

3:45 PM - 4:00 PM

High Tea

4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Intellectual Property Rights in the Digital Age

Examine the challenges of protecting intellectual property in the digital environment.

Discuss the impact of AI and emerging technologies on copyright and patent law.

Explore the role of IP law in promoting innovation and economic growth in the digital age.

7:00 PM onwards

Dinner at Surpura Bagh

Day 2 - April 06, 2025

Venue: Auditorium, NLU Jodhpur

9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Fintech and Medtech - Steering the Legal and Ethical Landscape

Discuss the legal and regulatory challenges related to financial technologies (Fintech) and medical technologies (Medtech).

Explore the ethical considerations surrounding data privacy, security, and algorithmic bias in these sectors.

Examine the role of IP law in fostering innovation and protecting investments in Fintech and Medtech.

10:30 AM - 11:00 AM

High Tea

11:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Digital Transformation in the Indian Context

Explore the impact of digital technologies on legal and administrative domains in India.

Discuss the challenges and opportunities of digital transformation in the Indian legal system.

Examine the role of government initiatives in promoting digital transformation.

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Lunch

2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Legal Analytics and Predictive Policing in India

Examine the use of advanced analytics for crime hotspot identification and predictive policing.

Discuss the legal and ethical implications of data-driven law enforcement.

Explore the potential of legal analytics to improve efficiency and fairness in the justice system.

4:00 PM - 4:30 PM

High Tea

4:30 PM - 5:30 PM
